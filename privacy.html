Cookies and Similar Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and login status. We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, remember your preferences, and understand how you use our services.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality of our website and services
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our site and services
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your choices and preferences
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your activity across websites for targeted advertising

Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, blocking cookies may affect the functionality of our website and services. You can also use industry-standard "Do Not Track" signals, though our website may not respond to these signals.

Similar Technologies

We may also use other tracking technologies such as web beacons, pixels, local storage, and similar technologies to track your activity and gather information about your usage patterns.
