About AI Testing Agency

Founded in 2020 by a team of software engineers and AI researchers, AI Testing Agency emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize how organizations approach quality assurance. Frustrated by the limitations of traditional testing methodologies, our founders recognized that artificial intelligence could fundamentally transform the industry. Starting with a small team in Silicon Valley, we've grown to serve hundreds of enterprises worldwide, processing millions of test cases daily and helping organizations deliver software with unprecedented speed and reliability.

We're committed to continuous innovation, maintaining the highest standards of security and reliability, and building lasting partnerships with our clients. Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our AI Testing Agency platform and services.

How does AI Testing Agency integrate with our existing CI/CD pipeline?

AI Testing Agency seamlessly integrates with all major CI/CD platforms including Jenkins, GitLab CI, GitHub Actions, CircleCI, and more. Our REST API and webhook support enable bidirectional communication with your pipeline. Setup typically takes less than 30 minutes, and we provide comprehensive documentation and dedicated onboarding support. The platform can be deployed on-premises or in the cloud, depending on your security requirements. Our integration team is available to assist with custom configurations specific to your infrastructure.

What types of applications can be tested with your platform?

Our platform supports testing for web applications (React, Angular, Vue, etc.), mobile applications (iOS and Android), desktop applications, APIs and microservices, and progressive web applications. We handle both traditional monolithic architectures and modern microservices-based systems. Whether you're building single-page applications, server-rendered applications, or native mobile apps, our AI-powered testing engine can adapt to your technology stack. We continuously expand our support for emerging frameworks and technologies.

How does the platform handle dynamic content and continuously changing UIs?

This is where our AI truly shines. Traditional automation frameworks break when UIs change, but our self-healing technology automatically adapts to UI modifications without requiring manual test script updates. Our machine learning algorithms understand the semantic meaning of page elements, not just their static identifiers. When a button moves or changes styling, our system recognizes it's still the same button and continues testing seamlessly. This capability alone saves teams hundreds of hours in test maintenance annually.

What is your data security and compliance posture?

Security is paramount to our operations. We maintain SOC 2 Type II certification, GDPR compliance, HIPAA readiness, and ISO 27001 standards. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We offer both cloud-hosted and on-premises deployment options. Our infrastructure is hosted on enterprise-grade cloud providers with 99.99% uptime guarantees. We conduct regular third-party security audits, penetration testing, and maintain comprehensive disaster recovery and business continuity plans. Client data is never used for training our AI models.

What kind of support and training do you provide?

We provide comprehensive support across all service tiers. This includes 24/7 technical support via chat, email, and phone for enterprise customers, detailed documentation and video tutorials, regular webinars and training sessions, and a dedicated customer success manager for enterprise accounts. We also maintain an active community forum where users share best practices and solutions. Our onboarding program ensures your team is fully equipped to maximize the platform's capabilities from day one, and we provide ongoing optimization recommendations based on your testing patterns.
